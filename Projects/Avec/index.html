Avec

Avec is a current project that aims to bring back "word-of-mouth" to the music world. Avec is a mobile app (soon available for Android and iOS!) that allows users to create unique profiles, consisting of a username and list of favorite musical artists. Users can see other users who are nearby, chat with them, add them as friends, and see their favorite artists. Coming soon!
