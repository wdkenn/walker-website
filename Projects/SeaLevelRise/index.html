Sea Level Rise

Currently in an early stage, this group project focuses on creating simulations of sea level rise globally. Having read many scientific articles (bibliography coming soon), we have noticed a lack of standardization in calculating sea level rise. Our group hopes to make a more general and appropriate algorithm to simulate sea level rise. We are taking a more algorithmic approach to a problem that has been looked at more closely through a Natural Science lens than a Computer Science one. The goal is to create a graphical representation of sea level rise when the user inputs a certain height. More to follow!
