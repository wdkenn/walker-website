About Me

I am a Computer Science and Music double major at Bowdoin College. More than anything, I love to create things and to solve problems. My majors are the perfect outlets for my passions. Programming allows me to create new mobile apps, websites, and more, while using problem solving to make these creations efficient and beautiful. Music gives me the tools to make new and interesting songs while challenging myself with new ideas and restraints.
Currently, I am the Station Manager at WBOR, Bowdoin College's radio station, overseeing and managing more than 100 DJ's, consisting of student and community members. In the past, I have worked as Concert Manager, organizing two concerts a year and bringing various artists to perform at Bowdoin.
In addition, I have been developing a teaching software for students of Afro-Latin Ensemble to learn drum rhythms and songs with their own computer.
I intend to continue work in both fields in a professional setting after college.
